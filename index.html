Basic Rules

Tips & Tricks to Improve

  • Avoid pegging the throttle — controlled speed prevents flips.
  • Use combined brake-and-forward inputs in tight spaces.
  • Tilt the vehicle with arrow keys to regain balance mid-air.
  • Set up speed before big jumps — under- or over-speeding causes failure.
  • Practice levels repeatedly; mastery comes from trial and error.
